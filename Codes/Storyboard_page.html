Theme

  • The website's color theme primarily features blueish green (turquoise) and whitewhich holds a few key attributes:
    • Professionalism: Turquoise exudes professionalism and trustworthiness, aligning well with content related to personal and career development.
    • Calmness: This color has a calming effect, enhancing the user's sense of tranquility while navigating the site.
    • Growth: Greenish tones symbolize growth and positive change, resonating with the theme of personal development.
  • White serves as a clean and minimalist background, promoting clarity and readability while maintaining a professional and versatile appearance. This color combination creates a visually appealing and user-friendly environment for visitors exploring your content. Consistency in using these colors throughout the website reinforces the chosen theme effectively.
